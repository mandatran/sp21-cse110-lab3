Chapter 1

The Pursuit of Almond Milk

April 5, 2021

Highlights

Research and rank the best non-dairy milk.

  1. Almond Milk
  2. Oat Milk
  3. Soy Milk
  4. Coconut Milk
  5. Cashew Milk

Lowlights

Research and rank the worst remaining milks. (1. being somewhat tolerable and 7. being the absolute worst)

  1. Lactose Free Milk
  2. Fat-free Milk
  3. 2% Milk
  4. Whipping Cream
  5. Whole Milk
  6. Half and Half
  7. Goat Milk

Thoughts

After thorough investigation, the milk from almonds triumphs.

The Acquisition of Almond Milk

April 6, 2021

Highlights

Today I had to figure out where the best place was to buy almond milk. Here's a list of some good places.

  1. Farmer's Market
  2. Costco
  3. Target

Lowlights

I also discussed the cons of milking your own almonds.

  1. Time consuming
  2. Messy
  3. Requires kitchen equipment you may not own, like a cheese cloth and processor.

Thoughts

I think it is best to acquire already-made almond milk rather than making your own.

Chapter 3

The Consumption of Almond Milk

April 7, 2021

Highlights

  1. Drinking almond milk.

Lowlights

  1. Running out of almond milk.

Thoughts

Make sure you always have a back up carton of almond milk.

Additional Thoughts

Almond milk is very versatile. It compliments coffee very well and is texturally similar to regular milk. It is also one of the healthier alternatives to regular milk and economically the best option as it is normally the cheapest non-dairy milk.
